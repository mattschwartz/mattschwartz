Trailer

Story

The game takes place in a post-apocalyptic world in which all life is presumed extinct. The unnamed protagonist, controlled by the player awakens in a city devastated by an unknown disaster and in order to find out what happened and who he is, he must uncover the truth.

Mechanics

After is a puzzle platformer, focused on telling the story of the Traveler and the city in which he finds himself through the environment and puzzles.

My Role

After was created by a group of five students, including myself, through UT's GAMMA game design program. The course was designed to be student-driven, allowing teams to design their own games with minimal constraints and guidance on the part of the instructors. My role in After was one of the two programmers and lead programmer for mobile direction for the game. More specifically, I:

  • Designed the puzzle game loop
    I programmed a set of Unity prefabs that allowed new puzzles to be set up quickly by defining how each individual piece was involved with other parts of the level
  • Designed a simple sound manager
    I worked with our sound engineer, Rob Luckfield, to develop a simple interface for adding and tweaking sounds in the game
  • Designed and implemented the mobile interface
    The instructors' gave additional credit for teams who implemented their game for mobile platforms as well. Ours was the only team to have done this. I developed the mobile interface, which was simply drag-to-move and touch-to-jump
  • Helped with the artwork
    I drew placeholder art and ended up finishing some first-pass artwork by our artist. The title scene artwork (also featured as the banner at the top of this page) was drawn by me
